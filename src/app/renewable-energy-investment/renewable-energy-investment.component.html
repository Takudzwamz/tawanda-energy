Tawanda Energy

Tawanda Energy is a developer, owner and operator of waste to energy renewable power assets in Zimbabwe and is positioned to become a key player in the energy transition sector in Africa. We are a proudly a local company committed to meaningful socio-economic development in the communities where we operate. Tawanda Energy invests in the most promising electricity generation and petrochemicals projects to ensure sustainable long-term investment returns.

Socio-Economic Development

Our mission is to ensure economic benefits for all our stakeholders and to help build an environment for sustainable job creation and socio-economic development.

Through strategic investments in the energy ecosystem, we aim to deliver superior returns for all our stakeholders and support sustainable development. Beyond our direct activities, we create and sustain jobs, and help improve the quality of life for the host communities where we operate.

Tawanda Energy welcomes enquiries from institutions, fund managers, private investors and other stakeholders who are looking for long-term investment in a renewable energy company with guaranteed returns.
