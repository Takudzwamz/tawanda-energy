Tawanda Energy

About Us

A society based on a green economy is Tawanda Energy’s dream. Fully aware of the need to adapt to the threat of climate change, we want to make a positive difference by making every individual, homeowner, or community to use renewable energy and fight energy poverty. We want to believe that every time we act, we take a small step towards greater change.

Moreover Tawanda Energy aims to make renewable energy available to all and give people the means to achieve their goals. A committed engineering team works very hard every day to convert waste into electricity and petrochemicals and renewable natural gas. Through our range of products and services, Tawanda Energy wants to make renewable energy products accessible and shows that yes it can be done

Lastly we believe in the sharing and transfer of knowledge. We think empowering our customers, especially regarding affordable access to RNG (Renewable Natural Gas) and diesel is paramount. Sharing our know how, educating our customers is in the end, what will really take us into a green economy and take renewable energy to a whole new level.

Circular Economy

In everything we do, we believe environment and waste management needs innovation. Our goal is to challenge the current “take and dispose” economy model. We believe in a circular economy were resources flow from one industry to another, creating long lasting material and energy loops.

Our products are made for consumers who think this way and want to show it to the world.

We do it by designing new innovative products and services that keeps materials in use, prevent pollution and produce energy. We develop new renewable energy generation processes that make the waste become a resource again. Because the way nature work is simple and circular. There is no waste only products.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a driving force for social, environmental, and economic benefits by producing gaseous and liquid climate-neutral energy carriers and are a project that aims to accelerate the development and introduction of climate neutral fuels into the Zimbabwean transport, construction, farming, and energy sector.

Our Mission

Our mission is to mitigate the negative impact of humans on the environment by introducing innovative and sustainable products and services to the market. Providing an end to end solution in the fields of waste water management, solid waste management, energy conservation and renewable energy production through continuous innovation. We strive to deliver the highest quality products at the right price.

Our Values

Tawanda’s values serve as the guiding light of our actions. They describe how we carry ourselves in the course of interacting with our customers and other stakeholders as we set about executing our business strategy in our business environment: Transparency, Honesty, Integrity and Accountability.

The Team

With dedicated energy and business professionals with years of experience and a strong commitment to customer satisfaction and service, Tawanda Energy offers customers and communities projects that will create real and sustained economic and operating benefits to fulfil unique requirements.
