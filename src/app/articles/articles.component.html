Services

The modern world would be nothing without the predictable and reliable manufacture, distribution and supply of energy. The energy industry is multi-faceted and diverse, comprising a wide range of disciplines and processes. Thanks to our knowledge and experience, Tawanda Energy is adept at running a smooth, profitable and reliable waste to energy business. We will produce the following :

50,000 Litres Diesel for the petroleum industry

We will convert all the hydrocarbon based feedstock to renewable diesel or sustainable synthetic oil or diesel. We will offer all fossil fuel dependent industries a viable and sustainable solution by making it possible to recycle all organic waste and plastics into renewable fuels liquids and oils.

The bio-refinery will have a capacity to produce up to 50,000 litres per day of diesel. The diesel will be sold petroleum traders and end-users at service stations. The diesel produced will meet the international standard of diesel. Our turnkey facility solution will encourage fossil based fuel independence using all kinds of organic and plastic residues as feedstock.

7000kg Renewable Natural Gas

Tawanda Energy will produce 7,000 kg of Renewable Natural Gas (RNG). The gas will be bottled and distributed using the existing infrastructure for Liquid Petroleum Gas and will be offered to the market a cheaper alternative to LPG.

Through the digestion of sewage sludge we will generate biogas. The biogas will be upgraded to a valuable commodity RNG (95% methane). Use for RNG include

● Automotive fuel

● Cooling/heating fuel

● In backup generators for electricity production

Feedstock diversification will allow Tawanda Energy to introduce bio-components that have the potential to dilute market risk and facilitate new market and business opportunities.

6.3 MW Electricity power plant

The waste to energy bio-refinery will also have a capacity for electricity generation the plant will have a capacity to generate 6,3MW/hr electricity.

The proposed waste to energy facility plans to sell its primary product of base load electricity under a long‐term contract with the Zimbabwe Electricity Transmission and Distribution Company (ZETDC).

30 Tonnes Green Coal

Tawanda Energy is working on pioneering the development and implementation of a strategy for the sustainable use green coal as a cheaper alternative for charcoal, coal and firewood.

Wood fuel is the main energy source in Zimbabwe and will continue to be used by rural and low-income urban households for the foreseeable future. Currently, an annual consumption of 6 million tonnes of wood is estimated, whereas the sustainable output of natural forests is only 4.6 million tonnes. This leads to a supply shortage in most rural areas and a loss of 330,000 ha of forest area per year. The green coal will also be used for in the following markets:

● Agriculture: Fertiliser supplement for broad acre pasture and cereal cropping, not including orchards, vegetables, vineyards or small agricultural endeavours

● Soil rehabilitation: Remediation of degraded soils

● Forestry: Fertiliser supplement for pinus radiata plantations

● Site (mine) rehabilitation: Remediation of degraded sites to aid re-establishment of plant growth

● Landscaping and minor horticulture: Composted product largely for urban landscaping and small scale horticultural use

● Building products: Cement and brick making, not including roof and floor tiles, specialised ceramics, minor refractory products and artificial aggregates

● Energy production: Direct combustion of bio-solids or biosolids derived products to produce energy

● Carbon sequestration:Land application of char derived from biosolids in order to gain carbon credits or similar
